1. Past of "Be" (was/were)

Use was para (I, he, she, it) e were para (you, we, they).
She was at home yesterday. (Ela estava em casa ontem.)
They were happy at the party. (Eles estavam felizes na festa.)
  1. She ___ at home yesterday.
  2. We ___ in Brazil last year.
  3. I ___ tired this morning.
  4. They ___ happy at the party.
  5. The cat ___ sleeping on the chair.

2. Comparatives

Adjetivo curto: -er than. Adjetivo longo: more + adj + than. Irregulares: good → better.
My car is faster than yours. (Meu carro é mais rápido que o seu.)
This book is more interesting than that one. (Este livro é mais interessante que aquele.)
  1. My car is ______ (fast) than yours.
  2. This watch is ______ (expensive) than the other one.
  3. The weather today is ______ (good) than yesterday.
  4. He is ______ (tall) than his brother.
  5. This movie is ______ (interesting) than that one.

7. Demonstrative Pronouns

Perto: this (singular), these (plural). Longe: that (singular), those (plural).
I like this car. (Eu gosto deste carro.)
Look at those birds! (Olhe para aqueles pássaros!)
  1. ______ (this) is my favorite song! (tocando agora)
  2. Look at ______ (those) birds in the sky! (longe)
  3. Can you see ______ (that) house on the hill? (longe)
  4. Are ______ (these) your books here on the table? (perto)
  5. Please, take ______ (this) folder. (entregando na mão)

8. Simple Present

Usado para hábitos, rotinas e fatos. Adicione -s ou -es ao verbo para he, she, it.
I work here. (Eu trabalho aqui.)
She works here. (Ela trabalha aqui.)
The Earth orbits the sun. (A Terra orbita o sol.)
  1. Look! The sun ______ (shine) brightly in the sky.
  2. Sarah ______ (go) to the gym every morning.
  3. Water ______ (boil) at 100 degrees Celsius.
  4. The children ______ (play) happily in the park.
  5. My parents ______ (watch) TV in the living room.

9. Simple Past

Ações finalizadas no passado.
  • Afirmativa: S + Verbo no Passado (-ed ou irregular).
  • Negativa: S + didn't + Verbo (base).
  • Interrogativa: Did + S + Verbo (base)?
She played the piano. (Ela tocou piano.)
She didn't play the piano. (Ela não tocou piano.)
Did she play the piano? (Ela tocou piano?)
  1. (Afirmativa) I ______ (visit) my grandparents last week.
  2. (Afirmativa) They ______ (go) to the cinema yesterday.
  3. (Negativa) He ______ (not eat) pizza last night.
  4. (Interrogativa) ______ you ______ (see) the movie?
  5. (Negativa) We ______ (not buy) a new car.

10. Future Tenses (Will / Going to)

Going to para planos. Will para decisões rápidas e previsões.
  • Afirmativa: S + will + Verbo (base).
  • Negativa: S + will not (won't) + Verbo (base).
I am going to travel. (Eu vou viajar - é um plano.)
She will not (won't) come to the party. (Ela não virá para a festa.)
  1. (Plano) We ______ (are going to) visit our family next Sunday.
  2. (Decisão) The phone is ringing. I ______ (will) get it!
  3. (Negativa) He ______ help you with the project.
  4. (Negativa) They ______ travel abroad next year.
  5. (Previsão) I think it ______ (will) rain later.

11. Present Continuous

Ação acontecendo agora: S + am/is/are + verbo-ING.
She is working now. (Ela está trabalhando agora.)
I am writing this lesson. (Eu estou escrevendo esta lição.)
  1. (Present) Look! He ______ (is reading) a book in the park.
  2. (Present) The children ______ (are playing) football at the moment.
  3. (Present) I can't talk now, I ______ (am studying) for my test.
  4. (Present) My father ______ (is working) in the garden right now.
  5. (Present) We ______ (are watching) a movie on TV.

12. Past Continuous

Ação em progresso no passado: S + was/were + verbo-ING.
I was sleeping when you called. (Eu estava dormindo quando você ligou.)
They were playing at 5 pm. (Eles estavam jogando às 17h.)
  1. (Past) I ______ (was sleeping) when you called me last night.
  2. (Past) He ______ (was watching) TV when the power went out.
  3. (Past) They ______ (were studying) for the test all evening yesterday.
  4. (Past) It ______ (was raining) when I left the house this morning.
  5. (Past) We ______ (were walking) in the park when we saw them.

15. WH Questions

Palavras usadas para fazer perguntas específicas.
  • What (O que/Qual) - When (Quando) - Where (Onde)
  • Who (Quem) - Whose (De quem) - Why (Por que)
What is your favorite color? (Qual é a sua cor favorita?)
Where do you live? (Onde você mora?)
Why are you sad? (Por que você está triste?)
  1. ______ is your birthday? (Quando)
  2. ______ do you live? (Onde)
  3. ______ is the President? (Quem)
  4. ______ car is this? (De quem)
  5. ______ are you crying? (Por que)

16. Phrasal Verbs

Combinação de um verbo + preposição/advérbio que cria um novo significado.
Wake up: parar de dormir.
I usually wake up at 7 AM. (Eu geralmente acordo às 7 da manhã.)
Get through: completar com sucesso.
I managed to get through my exams. (Eu consegui passar nas minhas provas.)
  1. It's time to ______ (acordar).
  2. Please ______ (arrumar) your room before guests arrive.
  3. Let's ______ (finalizar) this discussion and move on.
  4. We need to ______ (reduzir) the amount of sugar we eat.
  5. I need to buy groceries; we ______ (ficamos sem) milk.

3. Weather

Palavras comuns: sunny, rainy, cloudy, windy, snowy, cold, hot.
It's a sunny day. (É um dia ensolarado.)
It was very cold yesterday. (Estava muito frio ontem.)
  1. There are no clouds. It's a ______ (sunny) day.
  2. Take an umbrella, it looks ______. (rainy)
  3. Wear a jacket, it's very ______ (cold) outside.
  4. The leaves are flying. It's so ______. (windy)
  5. In winter, the mountains are often ______. (snowy)

4. Family Members

Vocabulário: father, mother, brother, sister, aunt, cousin, parents.
My mother's son is my brother. (O filho da minha mãe é meu irmão.)
My father and mother are my parents. (Meu pai e minha mãe são meus pais.)
  1. My mother's son is my ______.
  2. My father's sister is my ______.
  3. My aunt's son or daughter is my ______.
  4. My father and mother are my ______.
  5. My parent's daughter is my ______.

5. Whose

"Whose" pergunta sobre posse ("de quem"). Ex.: Whose book is this?
Whose car is this? (De quem é este carro?)
I don't know whose keys these are. (Eu não sei de quem são estas chaves.)
  1. ______ car is parked outside?
  2. ______ keys are on the table?
  3. I don't know ______ idea it was.
  4. ______ phone is this? It's ringing.
  5. She asked ______ jacket was on the chair.

6. Possessive Adjectives

Indicam posse antes de um substantivo: my, your, his, her, its, our, their.
This is my book. (Este é o meu livro.)
Their house is big. (A casa deles é grande.)
  1. This is ______ (my) book.
  2. Maria is looking for ______ (her) keys.
  3. The students love ______ (their) new teacher.
  4. We should finish ______ (our) homework.
  5. The dog is playing with ______ ball.

13. Object Pronouns

Recebem a ação: me, you, him, her, it, us, them. Ex: She called me.
She gave the book to him. (Ela deu o livro para ele.)
Can you help us? (Você pode nos ajudar?)
  1. Can you help ______, please? (me)
  2. I saw Sarah and gave the book to ______. (her)
  3. The teacher is talking to the students. She is talking to ______. (them)
  4. My brother is there. I'm going to talk to ______. (him)
  5. Do you want to come with ______? (us)

14. Intensifiers

Intensificam adjetivos: very, really, so, too. Ex.: very good, too expensive.
The movie was very interesting. (O filme foi muito interessante.)
This coffee is too hot to drink. (Este café está quente demais para beber.)
  1. The movie was ______ interesting. (very)
  2. This coffee is ______ hot to drink. (too)
  3. I was ______ tired that I went to bed early. (so)
  4. That was a ______ good presentation. (really)
  5. She is ______ excited about her vacation. (very)
